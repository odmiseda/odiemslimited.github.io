Who We Are

Odiems Limited is a registered Kenyan company championing smart agriculture and tech-driven solutions for farmers, agribusinesses, and cooperatives. With a strong foundation in mixed farming and IT integration, our aim is to bridge the digital divide in rural communities. From poultry and ornamental bird farming to agritech consultancy, our services are designed to empower, educate, and enrich.

What We Offer

  • Smart Farming: Modern methods for poultry, turkey, guineafowl & crops.
  • Digital Training: ICT support and tools for rural agribusiness owners.
  • Shylock Services: Safe, ethical credit for our trusted clients.
