Who We Are

Odiems Limited is a forward-thinking company based in Kenya, blending modern agriculture with cutting-edge information technology. We are committed to driving transformation in food production, rural development, and digital agriculture. Our mission is to empower farmers, agribusinesses, and communities through technology, innovation, and sustainability.

Our Vision

To be Africa’s leading digital and agricultural solutions provider, enriching lives and creating sustainable ecosystems through innovation.

Our Mission

To deliver high-quality agricultural products and intelligent IT solutions that solve real problems, increase productivity, and improve quality of life.

Core Values

  • Innovation: Constantly evolving to meet market needs
  • Integrity: We operate transparently and honestly
  • Impact: Improving lives through sustainable practices
  • Empowerment: Supporting farmers, youth, and tech-driven entrepreneurs

Our Business

We operate across several sectors including:

  • Agricultural production and supply chain management
  • Ornamental bird rearing (goose, turkey, guineafowl)
  • Mixed farming and agri-exports
  • Shylock micro-lending services
  • Digital platforms and information systems for farmers
  • Web and mobile technology solutions

Why Choose Us?

  • We are locally rooted, globally focused
  • Backed by experience in agriculture, finance, and IT
  • We build for impact, not just profit
  • We’re committed to Kenya’s food and digital future
