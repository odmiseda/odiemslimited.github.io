About Us

Odiems Limited – Agriculture | Innovation | Opportunity

Who We Are

Odiems Limited is a proudly Kenyan agribusiness company specializing in mixed farming, agri-tech, and rural development. Founded with a passion for transforming small-scale farms into profitable ventures, we provide products and services that support both new and experienced farmers.

Our Mission

To empower farmers and agribusinesses through affordable products, financing options, and access to technology-driven farming solutions.

Our Vision

To become East Africa’s most trusted platform for agricultural transformation by 2030.

Core Values

  • Innovation
  • Integrity
  • Empowerment
  • Sustainability
  • Community

"We believe that farming is not just a tradition—it’s a future full of opportunity. Through Odiems Limited, we aim to build wealth for individuals, families, and communities, one farm at a time."

— Denis Miseda, Founder & CEO
