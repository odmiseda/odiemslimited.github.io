About Odiems Limited

Empowering lives through agriculture and technology

Who We Are

Odiems Limited is a Kenyan-based company dedicated to promoting sustainable agriculture, innovative farming practices, and information technology solutions tailored for African farmers. Founded with a vision to empower farmers and rural communities, we bridge the gap between agribusiness and digital transformation.

Our Mission

To deliver high-quality agricultural products, services, and technologies that improve farm productivity, food security, and economic empowerment in Kenya and beyond.

What We Do

  • Support mixed farming including poultry, turkeys, geese, and guineafowls
  • Provide farm inputs such as quality seeds and animal feeds
  • Distribute agricultural information through our blog and digital platforms
  • Offer IT solutions for farm monitoring, record keeping, and data analytics
  • Promote youth and women participation in agribusiness

Why Choose Us

  • Registered and trusted Kenyan company
  • Strong blend of agriculture and IT expertise
  • Community-driven and focused on real impact
  • Transparent, ethical, and forward-thinking practices
