About Odiems Limited

Our Story

Odiems Limited was founded with a passion for merging agriculture and information technology to create sustainable, profitable, and modern farming practices in Kenya and beyond. From humble beginnings, we've grown into a brand that supports innovation, growth, and empowerment in rural and urban communities alike.

Our Mission

To lead the transformation of agriculture through technology, while creating opportunities for local farmers, entrepreneurs, and businesses to thrive in a digital-first economy.

Our Vision

Empowering communities by leveraging the synergy between farming and information technology to build a prosperous, food-secure future.

What We Do

  • Innovative agricultural production
  • Ornamental bird rearing
  • Technology-driven farming solutions
  • Agro-based digital content and education
  • Shylock and micro-financing services for farmers
