Our Services

Comprehensive Agricultural and IT Services for Sustainable Growth

1. Agricultural Consultation Services

Odiems Limited offers hands-on farm consultancy for both smallholder and commercial farmers. Whether you’re starting a poultry unit, looking to venture into mixed farming, or want to increase your farm’s productivity, our team provides tailored guidance.

  • Farm layout design and planning
  • Crop and livestock selection based on region
  • Poultry and ornamental bird rearing advisory
  • Soil testing, analysis and fertility recommendations
  • Input sourcing, budgeting, and operational planning

2. IT and Digital Services for Agribusiness

Our tech-driven solutions are designed to modernize agriculture through digital platforms. We build and manage farm-focused websites, offer mobile tools for farmers, and provide tech support that increases your digital visibility.

  • Custom website design for agribusinesses
  • Farm record-keeping apps and dashboards
  • SEO and digital marketing services
  • Domain registration and hosting setup
  • IT support for cooperatives, groups, and startups

3. Training and Capacity Building

Our training programs empower individuals, groups, youth, and women to create wealth through agriculture and ICT. We offer both physical and online sessions, practical field demos, and digital modules.

  • Poultry farming training (chicken, turkey, goose, guineafowl)
  • Agri-digital transformation training
  • Content creation for farmers (video, blog, social media)
  • Business planning and farm budgeting
  • Youth agri-entrepreneurship bootcamps

4. Farm Setup & Management Services

Starting a farm from scratch? Odiems Limited offers turnkey solutions from land selection to full-scale operations. We help clients set up ornamental bird units, poultry farms, and mixed farming projects.

  • Land selection and acquisition advisory
  • Poultry unit setup and bird supply
  • Irrigation and water harvesting systems
  • Solar solutions for remote farm locations
  • Seasonal project management contracts

5. Content & Blog Management for Agri Brands

Content drives visibility. We help agri-businesses, farmers, and institutions develop content strategies to grow traffic and attract clients online. We also automate blog publishing and optimize posts for SEO.

  • Weekly blog writing and editing
  • Visual content creation (photos, infographics)
  • Email marketing campaigns (Mailchimp setup)
  • Google AdSense optimization
  • Social media content calendars

6. Agribusiness Branding & Identity

Whether you’re just starting or scaling, we help define your agri-brand identity through:

  • Logo and brand kit design
  • Company profiles and pitch decks
  • Marketing collateral (banners, flyers, brochures)
  • Product photography & packaging consultation

7. Farmer Group Support Programs

We work closely with farmer groups, cooperatives, and SACCOs to improve member value. We offer structured programs on:

  • Digital group management systems
  • Bulk input sourcing and distribution
  • Loan readiness and enterprise planning
  • Group branding and digital presence

8. Custom Orders & Private Consultancy

Need a tailored project or private consultancy? We are open to local and international engagements. Contact us to discuss:

  • Private farm setup and family estates
  • One-on-one coaching
  • Digital transformation for NGOs and CSOs in agriculture
  • Strategic investment advisory
